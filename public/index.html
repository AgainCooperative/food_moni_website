About FoodMoni and our goals

FoodMoni is created to help grow the Food and Agribusiness sector to the most successful sector in Nigeria and Africa at large by providing food and agribusinesses with their most anticipated need, which is; access to FINANCE.

Our goal and vision for the Food and Agribusiness Sector will be gradually realized by interconnecting all players in the food and agribusiness value chain (from-farm-to-fork) which include; Investors, Farmers, Transporters, Marketers, Secondary Producers, Input Providers, Workforce and Consumers on the FoodMoni Ecosystem using the Chained-Circle-Methodology.
